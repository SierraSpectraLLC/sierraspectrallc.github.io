What We Do

Comprehensive Analytical Instrument Services

Expert support across the full service lifecycle — from scheduled preventative maintenance to emergency repair.

Mass Spectrometry Service

Expert maintenance, repair, and optimization for Sciex, Agilent, Waters, and Shimadzu MS systems. From routine maintenance to complex troubleshooting, our specialists ensure peak performance.

LC System Maintenance

Comprehensive service for HPLC and UHPLC systems including pumps, autosamplers, detectors, and columns. We provide preventative maintenance, repairs, and performance optimization.

Preventative Maintenance

Scheduled maintenance programs designed to maximize uptime, extend instrument life, and ensure consistent analytical results. Custom PM schedules based on your specific usage patterns.

Emergency Response

Rapid response services for critical instrument failures. Our team provides same-day or next-day onsite support to minimize downtime and get your lab back up and running quickly.

System Integration

Expert assistance with instrument networking, data systems, and software integration. We help optimize your lab's entire analytical workflow for maximum efficiency.

Service Contracts

Flexible service agreement options from basic coverage to comprehensive plans with dedicated engineer support. Customized to meet your lab's specific needs and budget requirements.

About Sierra Spectra

Service Excellence Built on OEM Experience

Sierra Spectra was founded with a singular mission: to provide analytical laboratories with service excellence that exceeds industry standards. Our team consists of OEM-trained engineers with extensive experience across all major analytical instrument platforms.

What sets us apart is our dedication to responsive communication, rapid service delivery, and a deep understanding of the critical role these instruments play in your operations. We don't just fix instruments — we partner with you to ensure your analytical capabilities remain consistently available and reliable.

Our founder's background includes years of experience as an engineer for major instrument manufacturers, giving us unique insight into the complexities of modern analytical systems. This expertise allows us to offer solutions that address not just immediate issues but underlying causes, resulting in more reliable instrument performance long-term.
